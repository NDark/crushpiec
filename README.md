Hi all, I am NDark, I made a prototype game with my dear friend Der Kasaki in 3 days at Lunar New Year Game Jam 2016. It's about "English Translation" and "Pixel Style Destruction", called "Crush Piec" (reading as crush piece without last e)。

We have screenshots at the end, also release apk here, and we open source our project at Github: https://github.com/NDark/crushpiec .

If you have any ideas about it, feel free to connect and reply.
