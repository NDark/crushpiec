Hi all, I am NDark, I made a prototype game with my dear friend Der Kasaki in 3 days at Zwei Zehnen Game Jam 2016. It's about "Three times rock, paper, scissors" and "Pixel Style", called "Geta Piece (分進不合擊)"。

We had screenshots at the end, also released apk here, and we open-source our project at Github: https://github.com/NDark/crushpiec .

If you have any ideas about it, feel free to comment.
